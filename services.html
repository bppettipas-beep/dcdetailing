FAQ

Common Questions

How long does a full detail take?▾

Typically 2–4 hours depending on the vehicle size and condition. We always give you an estimated time before starting, and we work efficiently without cutting corners.

Do you offer pick-up and drop-off?▾

Yes! We offer flexible drop-off options and can pick up your vehicle. You can also drop off your car in advance if that works better for your schedule. Just call to arrange.

How do I know what package is right for me?▾

Give Dishant a call at (506) 940-6494 and describe your vehicle's condition. He'll recommend the best option and give you an honest, transparent quote before anything begins.

Can you remove stubborn pet hair or smoke odour?▾

Absolutely. We use professional-grade hot water extraction and enzyme-based odour treatments that tackle even the toughest pet hair, smoke, and biological stains effectively.

Do prices vary by vehicle size?▾

Yes — sedans, SUVs, and trucks are priced differently due to the difference in interior and exterior surface area. The starting prices shown are for standard sedans. Call for an exact quote on your vehicle.
